How it Works?

Here is the simple step how localtoglobal works.

1. Download localtoglobal

Download and run a program on your machine and provide it the port of a network service, usually a web server.

2. Connect your service

Connect to the localtoglobal cloud service which accepts traffic on a public address.

3. Access from anywhere

Traffic is relayed through to the localtoglobal process running on your machine and then on to the local address you specified.
